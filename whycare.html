Why Care?

A history of abortion rights

Who does this affect?

Abortions are a basic healthcare need for millions of women, girls and people who can become pregnant.

  • According to the Guttmacher Institute, there were 14.4 abortions in the US per 1,000 women ages 15-24 (2020).

  • Forced pregnancy impacts everybody seeking reproductive and bodily freedom, but abortion bans and restrictions do not impact everyone equally.

    The burden hits hardest on those who already face systemic oppression including POC, undocumented, disabled, low-income, young, LGBTQ+, & rural folks.

    Fighting these laws is essential to the fight for racial, economic, and gender justice.
