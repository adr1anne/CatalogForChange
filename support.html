Abortion pills / At-home abortion

Mifepristone and misoprostol (aka abortion pills) are a relatively inexpensive, private, safe way to have an abortion early in pregnancy (before 11 weeks). These pills can be accessed from an abortion clinic, by prescription via telemedicine, or bought online without a prescription.

Regulations on abortion pills are constantly shifting and these laws are selectively enforced. These resources are available to help you navigate the at-home abortion process and assess any potential legal risk in your state
